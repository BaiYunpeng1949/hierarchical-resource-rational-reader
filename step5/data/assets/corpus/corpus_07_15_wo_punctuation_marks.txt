The Eiffel Tower in Paris France was completed in eighteen eightynine It stands three hundred twentyfour meters tall and attracts millions of visitors each year Originally criticized it has become a global cultural icon
The Great Wall of China stretching over thirteen thousand miles was primarily built during the Ming Dynasty It served as a defense system against invasions and raids Today it is a famous World Heritage site and a symbol of Chinas historical strength Tourists from all over the world come to see its greatness
The Amazon Rainforest often referred to as the lungs of the Earth produces about twenty percent of the worlds oxygen It is home to an incredibly diverse range of plants and animals including many species not found anywhere else The rainforest covers over five and a half million square kilometers spanning across nine countries in South America Deforestation and climate change pose significant threats to this vital ecosystem impacting global biodiversity and climate regulation
Albert Einstein born in eighteen seventynine in Germany is renowned for his contributions to theoretical physics His theory of relativity particularly the massenergy equivalence equation revolutionized our understanding of energy mass and the speed of light In nineteen twentyone he was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect Despite facing difficulties including fleeing Nazi Germany Einsteins work has had a lasting impact on science and modern technology influencing fields as diverse as quantum mechanics and cosmology
Marie Curie born in eighteen sixtyseven in Poland was a pioneering physicist and chemist who conducted groundbreaking research on radioactivity She was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize and remains the only person to have won Nobel Prizes in two different scientific fields Physics and Chemistry Curie discovered the elements polonium and radium and her work laid the foundation for the development of Xray machines Despite the challenges she faced as a woman in science during her time Curies contributions have had a lasting impact on medical and scientific advancements Her legacy continues to inspire scientists around the world
